NCAV = (Current Assets − Total Liabilities − Preferred Stock) ÷ Shares Outstanding. A stock trading below its NCAV is priced below what the company could theoretically liquidate for. Ben Graham's net-net strategy targets stocks below NCAV and has historically returned ~20% annually.
