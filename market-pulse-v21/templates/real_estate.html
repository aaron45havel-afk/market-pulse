What this means
How status is measured: a domino is marked ○ fallen when the indicator is ≥3% below its 18-month peak and its 6-month trend is negative (both conditions required — level + direction, per the EPB "wait for confirmation" rule). ◐ declining = one condition met. ● intact = neither. This differs from the YoY numbers shown on the forward-indicator cards below (which compare to 12 months ago), so a domino can be "fallen" even if YoY is positive — e.g. if it peaked recently and has since rolled over.
