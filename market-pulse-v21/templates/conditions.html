How is "Market Climate" scored?
A 0–100 composite of four Redfin signals. 0 = scorching seller's market (bidding wars, no inventory). 100 = arctic buyer's market (sellers cutting prices, listings sitting). Components, each equally weighted:
  • Sale-to-list ratio — <100% means sellers are accepting under-ask.
  • Listings with price drops — share of active listings that have cut their price.
  • Days on market — slower-moving inventory means buyers have leverage.
  • Months of supply — more inventory relative to demand = cooler market.
Anchors are fixed (not rebalanced each month), so a state's score moves because that state's fundamentals moved, not because its neighbors did.
